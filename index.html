NHS Programming Club

The NHS Programming Club is a club that allows fellow programmers to meet and work together to fix problems within our community using computer science. In the club, each member will program a specific part of a main, focused project, chosen by other club members. The programs the club creates are designed to help the community in some way. For example, one of the projects could be a re-design for a teacher's website or a program that helps students accomplish a goal more easily. Whatever the problem is, each club member will program and collaborate together using GitHub. Together, we will make the school and our community a better place.

In addition, the club members will have the chance to discuss concepts in different programming languages. Each club member will have the chance to share their own side-projects and ask programming questions to other members.
